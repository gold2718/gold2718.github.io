goldy's paleo chocolate chip cookies

Instructions for about one dozen cookies

I know, paleolithic folks likely did not have chocolate chips but they were probably dreaming about them.

Ingredients
IngredientAmount
Bob's Paleo Baking Flour3/4 cup
baking powder1 tsp
salt1/8 tsp (optional)
butter1/3 cup, softened
sugar (coconut, white, brown)1/2 cup
vanilla extract1/3 tsp
eggs1
chocolate chips2/3 cup

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Mix flour, baking soda and salt in small bowl.
  3. In a different bowl, beat butter, sugar and vanilla extract in a large bowl until just creamy.
  4. Beat egg and add to mixture.
  5. Gradually add flour mixture and beat until well-blended.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Chill dough for 10 - 30 minutes to reduce stickiness.
  8. Scoop or roll dough into balls, about two tablespoons each. Place 2 inches apart on prepared baking sheets.
  9. Bake for 11-13 minutes or until golden brown.
  10. Cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes.
  11. Move to wire rack to cool completely.
