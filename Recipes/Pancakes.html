goldy's buckwheat / almond pancakes

Ingredients

This recipe combines directions for making a batch of pancake batter mix with amounts for making different numbers of pancakes. Make the mix in advance, then follow the directions for making different numbers of pancakes.

Dry Ingredients
IngredientAmount
buckwheat flour1 cup
almond flour1/2 cup
coconut flour1/2 cup
3 Tbsp2 Tbsp1 Tbsp
baking powder3 tsp1.5 tsp1 tsp
coconut sugar (optional)2 Tbsp
Batter Ingredients
IngredientFor 5 - 6 4"For 1 - 2 5"
buttermilk1 cup1/2 cup3 Tbsp
butter12 Tbsp6 Tbsp7 Tbsp

Procedure

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Sift mixture into medium mixing bowl (can you sift almond flour?)
  3. Mix wet ingredients
  4. Gently stir liquid mixture into the sifted dry mixture. Let sit for at least 10 minutes.
  5. Spoon onto griddle set to 300 degrees or induction cooktop at 3.5.
  6. Cook each side until nicely brown (this recipe doesn't bubble).
  7. Serve with pure maple syrup!

goldy's almond pancakes

Ingredients

IngredientFor 9 - 10 largeFor 4 - 5 large
almond flour2 cups1 cup
baking powder3 tsp1.5 tsp
coconut milk1.5 cup1/3 cup
coconut sugar4 Tbsp2 Tbsp
eggs, possibly separated42

Procedure

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Sift mixture into medium mixing bowl (can you sift almond flour?)
  3. Mix wet ingredients (minus egg whites if separate)
  4. Gently stir liquid mixture into the sifted dry mixture. Let sit for at least 10 minutes.
  5. Beat egg whites until slightly stiff.
  6. Gently fold egg whites into mixture.
  7. Spoon onto griddle set to 300 degrees or induction cooktop at 3.5.
  8. Cook each side until nicely brown (this recipe doesn't bubble).
  9. Serve with pure maple syrup!

goldy's traditional pancakes

Ingredients

IngredientFor 9 - 10 largeFor 4 - 5 large
whole wheat pastry flour10 oz5
baking powder2 tsp1 tsp
lite salt1/4 tsp1/8 tsp
lowfat buttermilk1 5/8 cup3/4 cup
olive oil2 Tbsp1 Tbsp
fruit sweet6 Tbsp3 Tbsp
egg whites21
blueberries or chocolate chips~2 cups~1 cup

Procedure

  1. Mix flour, salt, and baking powder.
  2. Sift mixture into medium mixing bowl.
  3. Mix buttermilk, oil and fruit sweet.
  4. Gently stir liquid mixture into the sifted dry mixture. Let sit for at least 10 minutes.
  5. Beat egg whites until slightly stiff.
  6. Gently fold egg whites into mixture.
  7. Add blueberries or chips and mix in very gently.
  8. Spoon onto griddle set to 300 degrees.
  9. Cook each side until nicely brown (this recipe doesn't bubble).
  10. Serve with pure maple syrup!
