Quick Gluten-free Biscuits

Ingredients

IngredientAmount
Gluten free baking flour1/2 cup
Baking powder1 tsp
Salt1/8 tsp
Vinegar1 tsp
Eggs2

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 400F (200C)
  2. Combine the flour, baking powder and salt in a bowl
  3. Separate one egg and beat egg white
  4. Beat other egg and combine with vinegar
  5. Stir in egg mixture and beaten egg white with flour mixture
  6. Separate into two biscuits, place in greased skillet (spray or high-temp oil), and brush with egg yolk
  7. Bake at 400F (200C) for 15 minutes.
