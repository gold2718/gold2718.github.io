paleo pizza

This recipe is based on one from Bob's Red Mill

Ingredients

IngredientFor 1, 9-10" pie
Bob's Paleo Baking Flour1.25 cups (115 g)
eggs2
water2 TBSP
olive oil2 TBSP
baking powder0.5 tsp
salt1/4 tsp
pizza sauce~1/2 cup
shredded cheese~10 oz
other pizza ingredientsuse your imagination

Procedure

  1. Sift flour, salt, and baking powder into mixing bowl.
  2. Mix the eggs, water, and oil and add to flour mix.
  3. Allow dough to sit for a few (~5) minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 375o. Don't forget pizza stone if you use one.
  5. Roll out dough between two sheets of parchment paper
  6. Prebake pizza crust for 7 minutes.
  7. Add ingredients as desired (simple suggestion sauce then cheese).
  8. Bake the pie for about 5-7 minutes at 400o.
  9. Remove pie, slice and eat!

goldy's 'Dutch Baby' pizza

Ingredients

IngredientFor 1, 10" pie
Bob's Paleo Baking Flour1.5 cups (138 g)
baking powder1 tsp
eggs2
buttermilk2/3 cup (160 g)
butter1.5 Tbsp
toppingsto taste

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 425o
  2. Place 10" cast iron skillet in oven to preheat
  3. Mix eggs and buttermilk
  4. Mix flour and baking powder
  5. Stir flour mixture into liquid. Mix will be sticky but should be just runny enough to distribute in pan by tilting.
  6. Melt butter in skillet
  7. Pour mixture into skillet, tilting in a circular motion to distribute around pan. If possible, try to get the center a bit thinner than the edges.
  8. Bake at 400o for about 12 minutes or until center feels firm enough to hold up to toppings.
  9. Place toppings on pie and bake at 400o until ready. Total baking time should be around 20-22 minutes to give crust enough time to bake.

goldy's buckwheat pizza

Ingredients

IngredientFor 3, 9-10" pies
buckwheat flour155 g
almond meal205 g
eggs2
water2/3 cup
bread yeast1.5 tsp
sugar1 tsp
salt?? tsp
pizza sauce~1/2 cup
shredded cheese~10 oz
other pizza ingredientsuse your imagination

Procedure

  1. Add sugar to water and heat until it is about 105o F.
  2. Add yeast to water and stir. Allow to sit until yeast is fully awake (nice head of foam on top).
  3. Measure flours into mixing bowl.
  4. Add yeast mixture and eggs (beaten) to flour.
  5. Knead by hand as if making bread. Add water as neccessary (should use most of the water). Dough may be too sticky to use hands, using a spoon or spatula is okay.
  6. When the dough is almost ready, knead in the salt (if any).
  7. Allow dough to sit covered in a warm environment for about 1.5 hours.
  8. Preheat oven to 375o. Don't forget pizza stone if you use one.
  9. Divide dough in three (about 200g per pie) and roll out each piece until it fits a 9" pizza pan. Use parchment paper to avoid sticking
  10. Prebake pizza crust for 10-15 minutes. Remove and cool slightly.
  11. Add ingredients as desired (simple suggestion sauce then cheese).
  12. Bake pies for about 12 minutes. Bake directly on stone for crispiest crust. Use a pan with holes for a medium crust and a full-bottom pan for softer crust.
  13. Remove pie, slice and eat!

goldy's whole wheat pizza

Ingredients

IngredientFor 2, 9" pies
whole wheat flour14 oz
water1 1/4+ cups
bread yeast1.5 tsp
sugar1 tsp
salt1 tsp
pizza sauce~1/2 cup
shredded cheese~10 oz
other pizza ingredientsuse your imagination

Procedure

  1. Add sugar to 1/2 cup of water and heat until it is about 105o F.
  2. Add yeast to water and stir. Allow to sit until yeast is fully awake (nice head of foam on top).
  3. Measure flour into mixing bowl.
  4. Add yeast mixture and 3/4 cup of water to flour.
  5. Knead in mixer or by hand as if making bread. Add water as neccessary.
  6. When the dough is almost ready, knead in the salt.
  7. Allow dough to sit covered in a warm environment for about 1.5 hours.
  8. Preheat oven to 450o. Don't forget pizza stone if you use one.
  9. Punch the dough and divide in two. Knead a little to increase firmness (but not so much that it won't roll out).
  10. Roll out each piece until it fits a 9" pizza pan.
  11. Add ingredients as desired (simple suggestion sauce then cheese).
  12. Bake pies for about 12 minutes. Bake directly on stone for crispiest crust. Use a pan with holes for a medium crust and a full-bottom pan for softer crust.
  13. Remove pie, slice and eat!
