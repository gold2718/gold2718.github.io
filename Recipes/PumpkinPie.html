goldy's Healthy Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

IngredientFor 1 9 inch pie
pureed pumpkin (canned or fresh baked)15 oz
cocunut milk200 ml
ground cinnamon2 tsp
ground ginger1/2 tsp
ground nutmeg1/2 tsp
ground cloves1/4 tsp
ground cardamon (optional, not everyone likes this)1/8 tsp
slightly beaten eggs2
Maple Syrup or Agave1/3 cup

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 425o Farenheit.
  2. Mix together all ingredients. Make sure ingredients are well mixed but be gentle and try to avoid making bubbles.
  3. Gently pour mixture into well-buttered Pyrex pie plate. Leave at least a bit of room for expansion (although that should not be an issue with these proportions).
  4. Bake at 425o Farenheit for 10 minutes, then lower heat to 350o Farenheit and bake for another 35 - 40 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean from the center.
  5. Allow to cool before serving.

goldy's Traditional Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

IngredientFor 1 9 inch pie
pureed pumpkin (canned?)15 oz
sweetened condensed milk14 oz
ground cinnamon1 tsp
ground nutmeg1/2 tsp
ground ginger1/4 tsp
ground cloves1/4 tsp
slightly beaten eggs2
9 inch deep dish pie shell1

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 425o Farenheit.
  2. Mix together all ingredients (except pie shell). Make sure ingredients are well mixed but be gentle and try to avoid making bubbles.
  3. Gently pour mixture into pie shell. Stop just below the rim of the crust.
  4. Bake at 425o Farenheit for 15 minutes, then lower heat to 350o Farenheit and bake for another 35 - 40 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean from the center.
  5. Allow to cool before serving.
