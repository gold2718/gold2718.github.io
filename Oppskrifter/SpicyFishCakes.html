Spicy Fish Cakes

I use canned Chinook Salmon from American Tuna

Ingredients

IngredientFor 8 cakes
canned salmon12 oz
onion powder / Chopped onion1 Tbsp / 1/4 cup
cilantro1 Tbsp
chilli or curry powder1 tsp (2 for spicy)
garlic granules1 tsp
cumin1/2 tsp
mint1 tsp
bread crumbs or corn meal1/8 cup
red pepper flakes1/2 tsp (for spicy)
avocado oil2 Tbsp

Mix the spices, then add the salmon. Break the fish into small bits and work in the spices. Divide into 8 patties and let sit (can be refrigerated), 2-4 hours is ideal.

Heat the oil in the pan until hot but not smoking. Dip each side of the patties in bread crumbs and fry in the oil for 4-6 minutes on a side depending on pan temperature and how crispy you like the cakes.
