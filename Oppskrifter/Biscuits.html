Quick Gluten-free Biscuits

Ingredients

IngredientAmount
Gluten free baking flour1/2 cup
Baking powder1 tsp
Salt1/8 tsp
Vinegar1 tsp
Eggs2

Procedure

Note: If you are using large eggs, you may need more than 1/2 cup of flour. The dough should be sticky but not runny when you scoop it into the pan.

  1. Preheat oven to 400F (200C)
  2. Combine the flour, baking powder and salt in a bowl
  3. Separate one egg and beat egg white
  4. Beat other egg and combine with vinegar
  5. Stir in egg mixture and beaten egg white with flour mixture
  6. Separate into two biscuits, place in greased skillet (spray or high-temp oil), and brush with egg yolk
  7. Bake at 400F (200C) for 15 minutes.

Gluten-free Buttermilk Biscuits

Ingredients

IngredientAmount
Gluten free baking flour1.25 cup
Baking powder2 tsp
Sugar1 tsp
Salt1/8 tsp
Butter1/4 cup
Egg1
Butter3/8 cup + 2 tsp, divided

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 450Â° F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Cut in butter with a pastry blender until texture resembles small pebbles.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together egg and 3/8 cup buttermilk. Make a well in the center of the flour mixture and pour in the eggs and buttermilk. Stir until a soft dough forms. Dough will be slightly wet.
  5. Turn dough out onto a large piece of plastic wrap. Cover with another large piece of plastic wrap and roll or pat dough to 1/2 inch thick. Remove top piece of plastic wrap. Fold dough in half, then fold in half again. Cover dough with plastic wrap and roll or pat to 1 inch thick. Remove top piece of plastic wrap. Cut dough with 3-inch round biscuit cutter, pressing straight down (don't twist). If dough sticks, dip the cutter into gluten free flour between each biscuit.
  6. Place biscuits close together on the prepared baking sheet. Brush with remaining 1 Tbsp buttermilk. Bake for 18 minutes, or until tops are golden brown. Remove from oven and cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes.
