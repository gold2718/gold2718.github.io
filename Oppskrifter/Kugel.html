Ingredients

IngredientAmount
egg noodles1 lb.
sugar1/2 cup
eggs3
cinnamon1 tsp
vanilla1 tsp
salt1/8 tsp
raisins1/2 cup
optional: butter3 tbsp

Procedure

  1. Cook noodles according to package
  2. Beat eggs, gradually add the sugar until thick and light in color
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients (except for noodles), mixing between items
  4. Fold dough into the noodles
  5. Pour into 2 8"x8" or 9"x9" greased pans
  6. Bake for 45 minutes at 350F
