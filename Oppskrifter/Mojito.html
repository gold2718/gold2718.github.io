Mojito

Take 1 (from DrinkStreet)

Ingredients

IngredientFor 1
White Rum2 oz
Lime Juice1/2 oz
Sugar1 tsp
Mint Leaf6 whole
Soda Water2 oz

Procedure

  1. Put juice and sugar into a highball glass and stir until dissolved.
  2. Rub mint leaves on inside of the glass and discard.
  3. Fill with crushed ice and rum, stir.
  4. Top with soda water and garnish with a sprig of mint.

Take 2 (from "Mojito Man" at DrinkStreet)

Ingredients

IngredientFor 1
White Rum1.5 oz
Lime Juice1/4 lime
Sugar1.5 tsp
Mint Leaf6 whole
Club Soda6 oz

Procedure

  1. Put lime juice and rum with leaves and muddle (squish) together for at least a minute.
  2. Add sugar and mix well.
  3. Add soda.
  4. Rim glass with lime wedge and powdered sugar.

Take 3 (from Digs Magazine)

Ingredients

IngredientFor 1
Light Rum2.5 oz
Lime Juice1 lime
Simple Syrup1 Tbsp
Mint Leaf8 whole
Club Water6 oz

Procedure

  1. Place the mint leaves and 1 Tbsp. of the simple syrup (cooled) in the glass.
  2. Then squish it all around with a spoon (or whatever appropriate utensil you can find) for 20-30 seconds, until you can smell that good minty smell.
  3. Cut the lime in half. Squeeze the juice out from both halves into the glass, then drop one half into the glass.
  4. Pour in the rum and stir.
  5. Add plenty of ice, then top off the mixture with club soda. Garnish with a sprig of mint and enjoy!
