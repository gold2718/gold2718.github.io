goldy's Sunn Gresskar Pai

Ingredienser

IngredientFor 1 9 inch pie
pureed pumpkin (canned or fresh baked)425 g (15 oz)
cocunut milk200 ml
ground cinnamon2 tsp
ground ginger1/2 tsp
ground nutmeg1/2 tsp
ground cloves1/4 tsp
ground cardamon (optional, not everyone likes this) 1/8 tsp
slightly beaten eggs2
Maple Syrup or Agave1/3 cup

Procedure

  1. Forvarm ovnen til 220 oC (425 oF).
  2. Mix together all ingredients. Make sure ingredients are well mixed but be gentle and try to avoid making bubbles.
  3. Gently pour mixture into well-buttered Pyrex pie plate (eller paibunn). Leave at least a bit of room for expansion (although that should not be an issue with these proportions).
  4. Bake at 220 oC (425 oF) for 10 minutes, then lower heat to 175 oC (350 oF) and bake for another 35 - 40 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean from the center.
  5. Allow to cool before serving.

goldy's Tradisjonell Gresskar Pai

Ingredienser

IngredientFor 1 23 cm (9 inch) pai
pureed pumpkin (or canned) 425 g (15 oz)
sweetened condensed milk400 g (14 oz)
ground cinnamon1 tsp
ground nutmeg1/2 tsp
ground ginger1/4 tsp
ground cloves1/4 tsp
slightly beaten eggs2
23cm (9 inch) deep dish pai shell1

Procedure

  1. Forvarm ovnen til 220 oC (425 oF).
  2. Mix together all ingredients (except pai shell). Make sure ingredients are well mixed but be gentle and try to avoid making bubbles.
  3. Gently pour mixture into en paibunn. Stop just below the rim of the crust.
  4. Bake at 220 oC (425 oF) for 15 minutes, then lower heat to 175 oC (350 oF) and bake for another 35 - 40 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean from the center.
  5. Allow to cool before serving.

Hvordan bake et ferskt gresskar

  1. Forvarm ovnen til 190 oC (375 oF).
  2. Skjær gresskaret i to
  3. Øs ut frøene og trådete greier
  4. Olje lett innsiden
  5. Legg hver halve fruktsiden ned på en panne dekket med folie eller bakepapir
  6. Stek til skinnet er mykt nok til å stikke hull på med en gaffel - vanligvis fra 40 minutter til 1 time.

Glutenfrie Paibunn

Ingredienser

IngredientFor 1 23 cm (9 inch) pai
lyst glutenfritt mel200 g
smør100 g
sesam/rømme eller gresk yoghurt2 ss (Tbsp)
kaldt vann2-3 ss (Tbsp)

Procedure

  1. Bland sammen alle ingrediensene og kna godt til du har en jevn, fin deig.
  2. Dekk til deigen og la den hvile 30 minutter i kjøleskap.
  3. Trykk ut paibunnen i en smurt paiform eller kjevler den ut.
  4. Prikk hull med en gaffel.
  5. Forsteke den på 200 oC (395 oF) i circa ti minutter.
