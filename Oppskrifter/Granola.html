Ingredients

IngredientFor 9 cups
2.5 cupsRolled oats
0.5 cupcoconut oil
2 cupsseeds and/or chopped nuts (only use 1.5 cups if omitting sweetener)
1 cupdried fruit
1/3 cupsweetener (optional)
1 cupshredded coconut (optional)

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Line a large rimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil
  3. Combine dry ingredients (except fruit, if any)
  4. Combine oil with liquid sweetener (if any)
  5. Mix liquids with dry ingredients
  6. Lay out in baking sheet and bake for 25 - 30 minutes. Stir once or twice for even baking
  7. Mix in dried fruits
  8. Store or press together and let cool for larger chunks
