goldy's Cast Iron Skillet Cornbread

Ingredients
Ingredient10 inch pan8 inch pan
Bob's 1-1 Gluten Free or
Paleo Baking Flour		1 cup (148 g)5/8 cup (92 g)
Bob's Corn Flour1 cup (116 g)5/8 cup (72 g)
Bob's Medium Cornmeal1/2 cup (76 g)5/16 cup (48 g)
baking powder1 Tbsp2-3 tsp
sugar (coconut, white)5 tsp3 tsp
salt1/4 tsp (optional)1/8 tsp (optional)
Buttermilk1.4 cup (340 ml)7/8 cup (210 ml)
Apple Cider Vinegar1 Tbsp2 tsp
eggs21
butter1 Tbsp2 tsp

Procedure

  1. Place an 8 or 10-inch cast iron skillet in the oven and preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flours, cornmeal, baking powder, sugar and salt. Whisk to thoroughly combine.
  3. Next add milk, apple cider vinegar and eggs to the flour mixture. Whisk well to combine.
  4. Place butter in the hot cast iron skillet and swirl to coat. Pour batter into the skillet and bake for 30 minutes, or until golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Serve warm.
