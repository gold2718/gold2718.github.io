Chicken Breasts with Brandy

Ingredients

Ingredient4 servings
boneless skinless chicken breasts (Pounded and dusted lightly with flour)2 large
Olive oil1/4 cup
Butter2 Tbsp
Dry white wine1/2 cup
Garlic cloves, minced3
Pepperto taste
Finely chopped fresh parsley3 Tbsp
Brandy1/4 cup

Procedure

  1. Sauté chicken slowly in oil and butter.
  2. When browned, add wine, garlic, salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cook slowly for about 15 minutes.
  4. Add parsley.
  5. Before serving, heat brandy, pour over chicken and ignite.
